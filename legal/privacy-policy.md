## 2. Imported contacts — our binding commitments

When you import contacts, some of the people in your address book are **not TrustOS members**. For those persons we commit, as policy and product behaviour:

1. **We do not build profiles of non-members.** Their data serves only to map *your* relationships and is not used to score, advertise to, or evaluate them.
2. **We never message anyone from your contacts without your explicit, per-message action.**
3. **We do not show your contacts to other members.** Matching against members happens privately, using hashed identifiers.
4. **Erasure on request.** Any person, member or not, may demand erasure of data about them via **[public erasure form URL]**; verified requests are honoured by cryptographic deletion within [30] days, subject to legal retention duties.
5. You confirm, when importing, that you may lawfully share these details for this purpose. Withdrawing import consent deletes derived, non-member contact data on the same schedule.

## 3. Legal bases

Consent (itemised, per purpose, withdrawable as easily as given — Settings → Privacy); performance of our contract with you (operating features you invoke); legal obligation (KYC/AML, tax withholding, statutory requests); and, where a later jurisdiction recognises it, legitimate interests strictly for security and fraud prevention. We do not sell personal data, and we do not share it for third-party advertising.

## 4. The Trust Index — automated processing you can see and contest

The Trust Index is an automated, **contextual** business-reputation score computed from the factor categories published in the **Trust Index Policy** (verification, verified referral/opportunity outcomes, transactions, relationship quality, community contribution, consistency, knowledge contribution, peer vouches, integrity signals). Its effects on you are: routing priority for opportunities, access to trust-gated features, and the band (never the number) optionally visible to others.

Your rights over it: a **plain-language explanation** of your score and every material movement (in-app: Trust profile); **human review on appeal** (Grievance & Appeals Policy, resolution SLA [7] days); correction of underlying facts. We prohibit — contractually, for every member and API consumer — use of the Trust Index for credit, lending, insurance, employment, housing, or government-eligibility decisions.

## 6. Storage, security, and residency

Personal data of Indian members is stored and processed in **India** (our architecture assigns each member a home region). Security controls include encryption in transit and at rest, field-level encryption for sensitive identifiers, key management with per-user keys enabling cryptographic erasure, access controls and audit logging of PII access, and an incident-response program. No system is perfectly secure; we will notify the Data Protection Board of India and affected individuals of personal data breaches as the DPDP Act requires.
