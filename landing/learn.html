The visual field guide

How TrustOS works, in eight simple steps.

No jargon. Each idea gets one plain explanation and one picture. By the end you'll understand exactly how a name in your phone becomes money in your account — and why trust is the thing being measured.

01 · The idea

Trust is an asset. Attention is a liability.

Most apps are built to capture your attention — and attention is spent the moment it's given. TrustOS is built around trust, which does the opposite: it compounds.

A viral post spikes and fades within days. A trusted relationship, kept warm, is worth more every year — one warm introduction can close a deal ten cold emails can't. The whole platform is a bet on that second line staying above the first, forever.

Attention — spikes, then decays Trust — compounds

Illustrative. Over 12 months, bursts of attention net almost nothing; trust accrues.

Takeaway: TrustOS optimises the asset that grows, not the one that evaporates.

02 · The measure

Your Trust Index: one number, nine honest inputs.

Every member has a Digital Trust Index from 0 to 1000, sorted into five bands. It is not a vibe — it's a weighted sum of nine verifiable things.

The biggest slice is referral performance — did the people you vouched for actually convert? Money-verified signals count most; opinion counts least. Below is exactly how the 1000 points are allocated. Nothing here can be bought.

03 · The mechanism

Trust is earned in steps — and quietly lost if you coast.

Your score is not static. Verified good outcomes push it up; going silent lets it decay. It behaves like fitness, not a certificate.

Watch one member across a year: each settled referral and each vouch nudges the line higher, while a two-month quiet spell pulls it back. This is what makes the number mean something — it reflects what you're doing now, not what you did once.

One member's Trust Index over 12 months. Green markers add; the amber dip is decay from inactivity.

Takeaway: trust rises with verified action and fades without it — by design.

04 · The map

Your contacts become a navigable graph.

When you import contacts, TrustOS maps your relationships — who's close, who's fading, and who can introduce you to whom. Privately: nobody is messaged or profiled.

You sit at the centre. Each contact is a node, bigger the stronger the tie. The gold path shows a warm introduction route — the shortest trusted line from you to someone you don't yet know, through a person you both trust. That path is the product.

You (centre) → strongest tie → their contact. The brightest edges are your warm-intro paths.

Takeaway: a relationship you'd forgotten is often the shortest path to your next deal.

05 · The money

How a referral turns into a payout.

A referral moves through four verified stages. It only becomes money when the outcome is real — not when it's claimed.

A business funds a campaign. You refer someone who fits. When they take the qualifying action, then actually buy, the commission is held in escrow and released after a 14-day no-dispute window — through a double-entry ledger, to your bank. Every stage narrows honestly.

Illustrative funnel for 100 referrals on a ₹500 campaign. Only settled referrals pay — and count toward trust.

Takeaway: verified-or-nothing. Self-reported numbers move neither money nor your score.

06 · The scope

Referrals are one of ten opportunities.

The same trust pipeline that settles a referral can settle nine other kinds of value. That's why TrustOS is an Opportunity Network, not a referral app.

Six of these need no money to change hands — they build trust and warm the graph before the paid ones even have liquidity. Four are monetised today. Investment intros deliberately earn trust only, never cash (that's regulated territory).

07 · The daily loop

The Daily Success Formula, running for you.

Great networkers follow one rhythm every day. TrustOS automates the tedious parts of it so you only do the human part — showing up.

Seven steps, one loop. Each morning starts warmer than the last, because yesterday's outcomes made the graph smarter. Watch it cycle — this is the engine under the whole product.

Learn → Prepare → Prospect → Serve → Follow Up → Improve → Repeat.

Takeaway: the discipline is the product — the AI just removes the friction from every step.

08 · The gate

Why invitation-only — quality beats size.

An open network grows huge but dilutes: the more strangers you let in, the less any one connection is worth. TrustOS trades size for value per connection.

Every member is vouched for, so the average connection is trustworthy. The curve below shows the trade: as a network opens up, its raw size climbs but the value each link carries falls off a cliff. The realised value — size × trust — peaks with a curated, invited network.
